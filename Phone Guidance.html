INTRODUCTION

Mobile phones, also called cell phones, are portable devices that let people talk and send messages without needing to be in one place. They were first introduced in the 1980s and have changed a lot since then. Mobile phones have become a big part of daily life because they help people stay connected, find information, and make tasks easier.
