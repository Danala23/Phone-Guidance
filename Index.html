IMPORTANCE

Leading mobile phone usage in schools is a task filled with dilemmas but it has its significance considering the benefits and cons that comes with embracing the technology. Phones have the benefit of accessing educational materials and opportunities enhances creativity and interactive education, as well as e-mailing, and downloading number of educational apps. In the right hands, they serve to augment the process of imparting digital knowledge among pupils and educating them about proper handling of the internet and phones. Schools help discourage students from indulging in distractions, misuse, abuse or phone addiction through strict implementation of order and time management through set regulations that must be adhered. In cases such as these, it is important for schools to issue guidelines that would help minimize cyberbullying.
